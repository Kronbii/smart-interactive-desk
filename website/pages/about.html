Our Story

The conviction in our mission came after being in the sign language education industry for two decades as pioneers and innovators. Thus, 2Sign was born out of necessity, facing challenges like accessibility barriers and communication gaps head-on.

At 2Sign, we understand that such problems cannot be solved without new innovative approaches. Thus, we use cutting-edge technology and methodologies to simplify sign language learning for everyday communication.
